**📌Business Problem**
94 employees across 3 locations with a -14% performance drop 
(82% → 71%) from 2019 to 2020. Goal: identify underperforming 
locations/departments relative to their cost.

**📊 Data Structure**
**Employee data:** job title, location, education level, hire year, age, experience years
**Financial data:** min/max/avg salary per location & job title
**Performance data:** PER scores for 2019 & 2020 + delta

**🧹Data Cleaning**
Handled missing values in salary and performance fields
Standardized location names and job titles
Checked for outliers in salary and age columns

**🛠️Tools Used**
**Power Query** (M Language), Pivot tables, and all dashboard visuals

**💡Key Insights**
1. Alexandria had the largest performance drop **(-16%)** despite being the biggest team (46% of staff) 
2. Tanta has the **smallest team (17%) but best performance** → expand 
3. Alexandria has the most experienced staff yet the **lowest performance** experience isn't converting to results 
4. Sales Reps in Alexandria = 18 out of 46 employees, but performance is low 
5. Pay equity needs restructuring large gap between min/max salaries 
6. 58.5% of employees hold a bachelor's degree entire sales team holds a diploma 
7. 2016 was the peak hiring year (18.09%)

**✅Recommendations**
1. **Expand Tanta's team** high performance, low cost = **best ROI**
2. **Restructure Alexandria's sales reps** especially Sales Reps, high headcount low output
3. **Reallocate employees** based on performance data, not headcount
4. **restructure salary bands** to achieve pay equity
5. **Invest in upskilling** the diploma-holding sales team
6. **Investigate the 2020 drop** external factors ( COVID) vs. internal issues
